Your Mobile Business Launch Canvas

A simple, step-by-step guide to formalize and grow your phone repair and sales venture.

Designed for the visual learner. Just follow the flow!

1. BUILD THE FOUNDATION

Set up the legal & financial backbone.

Legal Structure (LLC Recommended)

Protect your personal assets and gain credibility for sales.

  • Choose a Unique Business Name.
  • Register with Companies Office of Jamaica (COJ).
  • Get Business TRN.
Objective: Personal Protection & Formal Status

Financial Setup & Profit Plan

Separate money, track everything, plan your profit.

  • Open a dedicated Business Bank Account.
  • Set up a simple Bookkeeping Spreadsheet.
  • Define **Pricing:** `Cost + Labour/Margin = Price`.
  • Research Local Market Rates for competitive pricing.
Objective: Clear Finances & Guaranteed Profit

2. MASTER OPERATIONS

Run the business smoothly & ensure quality.

Sourcing & Inventory (Parts & Phones)

Find reliable suppliers for quality components & phones.

  • Identify **Wholesale Suppliers** for refurbished phones.
  • Source **Repair Parts** (local/international).
  • **VET SUPPLIERS:** Start small, check return policy.
  • Manage stock: Common parts in hand, others on-demand.
Objective: Consistent Supply & Good Prices

Quality Control & Customer Trust

Ensure every device is top-notch, especially "as-is" sales.

  • **21-Point Inspection Checklist** for ALL refurbished phones.
  • Document pre-repair condition with customer.
  • **TRANSPARENCY:** Clearly state "Sold As Is" for phones.
  • Offer "good faith" 24-hr guarantee for phones.
Objective: Deliver Excellence & Build Confidence

3. FUEL GROWTH

Attract customers & expand your reach.

Digital Presence (Build Trust)

Get found online, showcase your work.

  • **Google Business Profile:** (FREE & CRITICAL)
  • Instagram & Facebook Business Pages (Visuals!).
  • Dedicated Business Phone/WhatsApp.
  • **GET REVIEWS!** (Ask every happy customer).
Objective: Online Visibility & Credibility

Marketing & Sales (Get Customers)

Spread the word and make sales.

  • **Word-of-Mouth:** Tell everyone you know!
  • **Facebook Marketplace:** Great for selling phones.
  • Post in Local Community Groups (repair offers).
  • Launch a simple **Referral Program**.
Objective: Consistent Inquiries & Sales
"Depicting the path to success. One step at a time."
