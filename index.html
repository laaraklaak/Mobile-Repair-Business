The Repair Business Workflow

A successful business isn't just about the repair itself. It involves a structured process from acquiring parts to marketing your services. Following these steps ensures a professional and efficient operation.

1

Skill Development

Start by practicing on old or broken phones. Buy cheap, locked, or damaged devices from eBay or local marketplaces to disassemble and reassemble without risk.

2

Acquire Tools & Parts

Purchase your essential toolkit. For parts, find a reliable supplier. Start by ordering parts on-demand for specific jobs to minimize initial inventory costs.

3

Marketing & First Customers

Begin by offering repairs to friends and family. Create a simple Facebook Business Page or post on local marketplaces to attract your first clients.

4

Pricing & Profit

Research local repair prices. A common strategy is to charge the cost of the part plus a labor fee (e.g., $50-$100). Be transparent with your pricing.
